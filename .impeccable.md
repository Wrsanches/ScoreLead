## Design Context

### Users

ScoreLead serves B2B sales teams, agencies, founders, and growth operators who
need to find, qualify, and contact companies efficiently. Public-facing flows
should help prospective customers, current users, and potential partners get a
clear answer or reach the team without friction.

### Brand Personality

Focused, capable, approachable. ScoreLead should feel like a sharp operator:
confident about the work, direct in its language, and human when people need
help.

### Aesthetic Direction

A refined utilitarian dark interface with restrained emerald accents, precise
spacing, and strong editorial hierarchy. It should feel fast and purposeful,
not flashy or futuristic. Maintain the established dark ScoreLead system and
avoid decorative gradients, excessive glow, glass-heavy surfaces, and generic
card grids.

### Design Principles

1. Make the next action obvious and keep copy concise.
2. Use contrast, typography, and spacing before adding decoration.
3. Treat accessibility, autofill, keyboard use, and responsive behavior as
   core interaction requirements.
4. Use emerald sparingly to signal progress, focus, and successful outcomes.
5. Preserve a human tone in support, validation, and empty states.
